• 1928: The Art News

  • This was an advertisement made by the previous owner and art dealer Norbert Fischmann as a Lucas Cranach






  • 1931: International Studio

  • Describes painting as a handsome version of Lucas Cranach's Suffer the little chidlren to come unto Me owned by Norbert Fischmann










  • 1939: Auction by Adolf Weinmuller

  • Sold by the German art dealer Adolf Weinmuller as Cranach Nachfolger






  • 1963: Restoration report

  • This restoration report is for a matching Lucas Cranach the Elder painting in Larvik, Norway. The report states the Fischmann Cranach was used as a reference during the restoration since a head was missing on the Norway Cranach.
